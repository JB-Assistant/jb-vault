## Key Notes

- **Who to DM first:** Start with people who have posted about AI agents recently but sound frustrated or seeking better results. They're primed.
- **Social proof:** Once you have 1-2 testimonials, add them to the DM pitch. "I just did this for [Name] and they said..." is powerful.
- **Booking:** Set up a Calendly or similar. The friction of "book a time" is higher than "reply yes to this DM."
- **Objection handling:** If someone says "I already know how to do this" — "No problem, totally get it. If you ever want a second pair of eyes on it, I'm around."
- **Don't oversell:** The message works because it doesn't oversell. "If it's not useful, I'll tell you" is the closer.
