### Part 2: Skills (4 Ready-to-Use Templates)

Drop these into your agent and they're activated automatically:

| Skill | What it does | When it fires |
|---|---|---|
| **meeting-prep.md** | Pulls context from your calendar, CRM, and notes → builds a one-page briefing before every meeting | "prep for my meeting with X" |
| **content-research.md** | Runs 5+ structured searches → saves a sourced research brief organized by theme | "research [topic] for my next article" |
| **client-brief.md** | Turns a raw client request into a structured, client-ready brief with open questions | "I just got off a call, brief this out" |
| **slide-builder.md** | Runs the full pipeline: research → brief → Gamma outline → generated deck | "build slides for [topic]" |

## How It Works

```
1. Pick your persona pack (or all 4)
2. Fill in the [BRACKETS] — the agent does 80% of this for you
3. Save to your context folder
4. Drop skills into your skills folder
5. Your agent now knows who you are and has specialist capabilities
```

**Time to set up:** ~20 minutes.
**What changes:** Your agent stops sounding generic and starts sounding like you.
