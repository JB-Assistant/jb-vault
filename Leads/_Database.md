## How It Works
1. **Lead Hunter** runs daily at 8am EST
2. New leads saved to `Leads/Daily/YYYY-MM-DD.md`
3. Leads tagged by status and product
4. Review Unqualified → move to Qualified
5. Outreach via Qualified → Contacted
6. Track responses in In Conversation
7. Log wins in Converted
