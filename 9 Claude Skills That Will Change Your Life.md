## The 9 Skills

### #1 — Morning Analyst
Structured daily brief: industry news, market updates, priority tasks, calendar prep. Best used as a scheduled task.

### #2 — Voice Loader
Loads tone, style, and voice so content sounds like you. Works across Claude, Manus, Perplexity.

### #3 — SOP Engine
Trains Claude on your standard operating procedures. Connected to Notion/CRM for real-time SOP doc access.

### #4 — Research Format
Defines how you want information structured, sourced, and delivered. Author uses PDF format.

### #5 — Content Distribution Engine
Turn one piece of content into a full distribution engine. Author's most-used skill.

### #6 — Strategic Advisor
Role, goals, current situation → tailored strategic advice. Compounds over time. Update every 2-3 months.

### #7 — Prompt Generator
Builds optimal prompts for any AI model (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini). Use when feeling friction with outputs.

### #8 — Code Shipper
Idea to prototype fast. Prioritizes shipping over perfection. Trigger: "I want to build [idea]."

### #9 — Thinking Partner
Most valuable skill according to author. Challenges you, surfaces blind spots, forces clarity. Weekly use in business.

## Top 3 by Impact
#9 (Thinking Partner), #5 (Content Distribution), #1 (Morning Analyst)
